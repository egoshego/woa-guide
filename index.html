The game in 30 seconds

What is this?

Bosses are the heart of it. Players team up and hammer a shared boss — every hit earns you XP and a slice of its Coins Jackpot (split by how hard you hit). And every boss you bring down burns tokens forever, shrinking the supply.

Duels are 1-on-1. Step into the Arena, pick an opponent, and fight a pure-skill match — no gear advantage. Fight for nothing, or wager real $Mr. Eggplant and take the winner's pot.

Warriors are tanky, mages hit hard but fragile, rogues rely on luck for crits and dodges.

📊

Stats & Levels

▶
  • ATK — damage you deal per hit.
  • DEF — reduces damage you take.
  • HP — how much you survive before a knockout.
  • Luck — raises crit chance and your odds to dodge boss attacks.

Every level: +1 ATK, +1 DEF, +5 HP. Luck doesn't grow with level — it comes from your class and gear. Effective stat = base + class + level + gear.

🗡️

Combat & Weapons

▶

Every strike has two phases. Hit roll (d20): roll high enough to land — a natural 20 always hits and deals double. Damage = weapon dice + ATK − the boss's DEF (always at least 1).

📈

Earning XP

▶
  • Fighting bosses is your main XP source — steady hits, up to 200 strikes/day.
  • Invite friends: +250 XP each (first 30 recruits).
  • New players start with a +100 XP welcome bonus.
🔥

Coins, Jackpot & Burning

▶

Every boss carries a Coins Jackpot, split among raiders in proportion to damage dealt — hit harder, earn more.

Raiding is the whole point: every boss you bring down burns $ANALTENA and $Mr. Eggplant forever. For each boss, the creators set aside a pool that's permanently burned on-chain the moment it dies. Raid hard, burn more, make the tokens scarcer.

🏟️

Arena Rules

▶
  • Rounds last up to 3 minutes — pure skill, no gear advantage.
  • 2 shields per match. Idle 90s and you forfeit (warned at 60s).
  • Wager 30k–60k $Mr. Eggplant; winner takes the pot minus a 2% rake.
  • Arming a duel costs 0.006 XNT — covers paying out the winner and moving the rake to the burn.

Ready to play for real

Get In

Free play needs nothing. To wager $Mr. Eggplant, connect a wallet — four steps.

🍆

This is a guide, not the game. It won't connect your wallet or move any tokens — it just shows you how. You do the real thing inside the World of Analtena app.

1

Connect your wallet

▶

In the World of Analtena app, tap Connect Wallet. Your wallet app pops up — tap Approve. Done. The app shows your address.

Connecting just lets the game see your address. It can't move your tokens — every duel asks you to approve separately.

2

Get your tokens

▶

You duel with $Mr. Eggplant 🍆 — that's what you wager and win. You also need a small amount of XNT to pay the network fee each time you arm a duel.

Wager: 30,000–60,000 $Mr. Eggplant Fee: 0.006 XNT per duel

Hold a little XNT spare so a duel never fails mid-arm over gas.

3

Challenge or accept a duel

▶

Pick an opponent, set your wager between 30k and 60k, and send the challenge. Or accept one someone sent you.

Wait for the green light. Only authorize your stake after your opponent has accepted — no point locking tokens for someone who never shows.

🍆 Mr. Eggplant's tip: accept first, arm second
4

Fight & win the pot

▶

Rounds last up to 3 minutes. You get 2 shields per match to block at the right moment. Stay active — idle for 90 seconds and you forfeit (you're warned at 60s).

Winner takes the pot minus a 2% rake. On a draw, both get refunded their share minus the same rake. The rake is burned forever.
